How To Use

Starting a game will generate a random turn order. Once your turn ends, choose how much memory you gave your opponent. This is your exhaust score. When the last player in the turn order starts his turn, he will be moved to the top and a new turn order will be generated for the remaining 3 players based on exhaust scores.

In the case there are multiple player with the same exhaust score, the player with the higher lower total exhaust score will go before the other. In the case that there are multiple players with the same exhaust AND total exhaust, those players turn orders will be randomly determined

Total exhaust is the sum of your exhaust score and the exhaust score of the previous turn player.
